Delivery Requirements

What We Need From You

  • Accurate Address: Please provide complete and accurate delivery address
  • Contact Information: Valid phone number for delivery coordination
  • Access Instructions: Any specific instructions for finding your location
  • Availability: Someone should be available to receive the delivery

Important Notes

  • We will attempt delivery up to 3 times
  • If no one is available, we'll leave the package in a safe location (if possible)
  • We are not responsible for items left unattended
  • Please ensure someone is available during the delivery window
